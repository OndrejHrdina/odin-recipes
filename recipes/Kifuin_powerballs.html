Kifuin Powerballs

This protein-rich snack is very versatile. It can be eaten as-is, in a bread or in a sauce.

Ingredients

  • minced brawnbeans
  • half of your mince volume's worth of breadcrumbs
  • a touch of any milk
  • flax meal
  • Kifuin monastery spice mix
  • oil of your choice

Steps

  1. Combine everything together while adding milk, until you have a smooth, firm mixture
  2. Form mixture into balls
  3. Fry balls in hot oil until brown-red
  4. Enjoy hot or cold, as-is or in a bread!
